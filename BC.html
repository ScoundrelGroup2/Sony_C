Note!

  • According to HR clarification of new Travel Policy for one biz trip to multi-country, per diem payment will be based on next destination of departure date for calculation. 根據HR的解釋，若一趟出差包含多個國家，跨國交通移動日的日當以當日下一個目的地計算。
  • If a staff apply one biz trip from May 11 to 15 to multi-country, then per diem for May 13:
  • Scenario A) : 5/11 ~ 13 TPE to China (per diem USD 60) 5/13 ~ 15 China to JPN (per diem USD 70) Then 5/13 per diem is USD70.
  • Scenario B) : 5/11 ~ 13 TPE to JPN (per diem USD 70) 5/13 ~ 15 JPN to China (per diem USD 60) Then 5/13 per diem is USD60.
